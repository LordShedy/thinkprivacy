VPN & DNS

Use: Whenever you're connected to the internet. Most importantly when on any public wifi.

Product Description Location Price
NordVPN* Popular VPN service based out of Panama. Feature rich, fast, and really affordable. Panama $6.99/mo for the 1-year plan
ProtonVPN* Makers of ProtonMail, which is created by scientists from CERN. Fast servers. Switzerland Free - $24/mo
Mullvad Based out of Sweden with blazingly fast servers all over the world. Their token based account system is one of the most secure and anonymous VPN services on the market. Sweden $5/mo
IPVanish* Popular VPN service, especially among the Amazon Firestick crowd. Great speed and easy to use. Based in the US. Switzerland $6.49/mo for the 1-year plan
Express VPN* One of the larger VPN providers known for great connection speeds and servers all around the globe. British Virgin Islands $6.67/mo
Tunnel Bear* Popular US based VPN service with lots of fast servers. US Free - $9.99/mo
