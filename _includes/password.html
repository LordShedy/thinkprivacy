Password Managers

Name Description Skill Level Cost
Bitwarden So easy to use, cross-platform (Mac, Windows, Linux, iOS, Android) application. The web app and browser plugin are fantastic and the password generator is incredibly helpful. Beginner Free
KeePassXC The thing computers can do best is storing information. You shouldn't waste your time trying to remember and type your passwords. KeePassXC can store your passwords safely and auto-type them into your everyday websites and applications. Intermediate Free
1Password Without a doubt, this may be the best password management solution around. It is, however, the most expensive as well, but often times they say you get what you pay for. If you have the budget for it, consider giving this one a spin. Beginner $2.99/mo
Dashlane Easy to use, works on multiple platforms. The browser plugin makes creating brand new passwords a snap, and it makes changing existing passwords even easier. Certainly one of the best bangs for your buck. Beginner Free
LastPass One of the best known passowrd managers out there and even one of the least expensive. They do offer some desktop apps, but seems to work best when using it in the browser or mobile device. A lot of the same features as Dashlane. Beginner Free
